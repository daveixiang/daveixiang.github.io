C declaration Right-Left rule

2016-04-27
clan

The "right-left" rule is a completely regular rule for deciphering C declarations. It can also be useful in creating them. First, symbols. Read * as "pointer to" - always on the left side [] as "array of" - always on the right side () as "function returning" - always on the right side as you encounter them in the declaration. STEP 1 ------ Find the identifier. This is your starting point.
